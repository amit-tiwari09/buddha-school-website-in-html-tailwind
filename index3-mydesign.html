About Our School

Where Wisdom Meets Education

Buddha School, nestled in the sacred birthplace of Lord Buddha in Lumbini, has been a beacon of holistic education since 1999. Our institution uniquely combines the timeless wisdom of Buddhist teachings with contemporary academic excellence.

We believe in nurturing not just the intellect, but also the heart and spirit of every student. Through mindfulness practices, ethical teachings, and rigorous academics, we prepare our students to be compassionate leaders of tomorrow.
