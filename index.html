About Our Institution

Empowering Future Leaders Through Quality Education

Founded in 2064 B.S. at Kanchan Rural Municipality-3, Haraiya, our institution stands as a beacon of academic excellence and community development. Affiliated to Tribhuvan University, Nepal, we pride ourselves on being one of the premier community-based academic institutions in the region.

Established by distinguished academicians and dedicated social activists, we have consistently maintained our commitment to providing quality education that transforms lives and builds stronger communities.
